Welcome to NutriHelix

NutriHelix is a company at the forefront of personalised nutrition.

Our team of experts includes nutritionists specialised in the field of genotype- based personalised nutrition and wellness coaches who work together to provide a comprehensive approach to health and wellness. We use the latest technology and research to ensure that our recommendations are based on the most up-to-date and accurate information available.

In addition to genetic testing services, we offer a range of educational resources to help individuals better understand their genetic makeup and how it can impact their health. Our team is committed to providing personalised support to each and every client, ensuring that they have the tools they need to take control of their health and live their best life.

Our Mission

Nutrition is an essential part of a healthy lifestyle, and it plays a vital role in preventing several chronic diseases such as cardiovascular disease, cancer, and diabetes. As these diseases become increasingly common, the importance of nutrition in preventing and managing them has never been more critical. Scientific evidence demonstrates that proper nutrition can help address modifiable factors such as maintaining a healthy weight, reducing inflammation, and optimising blood sugar and cholesterol levels. One-size-fits-all approach has often shown to be insufficient in achieving optimal health and wellbeing as each individual has unique characteristics and needs. However, by tailoring nutritional recommendations to each individual, we can optimise health outcomes and help prevent chronic diseases.

At NutriHelix, we understand that each person's genetic code is unique, and this information can provide valuable insight into their respond to diet, exercise, and lifestyle changes that can help prevent disease and promote optimal health.

Our mission is to provide the most updated personalised and actionable nutritional advice based on an individual's unique genetic makeup to optimise people health and wellness in a sustainable and socially responsible way.

By tailoring nutrition plans to an individual's unique needs, we can also contribute to environmental sustainability in multiple ways. For instance, by preventing the development of chronic diseases, we can reduce healthcare costs and alleviate the burden on healthcare systems, ultimately resulting in a more sustainable healthcare system. Additionally, personalised nutrition can help reduce food waste by ensuring that individuals purchase and consume only the foods they need, minimising the overall amount of food waste.


Our Vision:

To provide the most precise and personalised nutritional advice based on an individual's unique genetic makeup, biological characteristics, lifestyle, and preferences to help individuals achieve optimal health and wellbeing.


Our Hope:

To empower individuals to take control of their health and make sustainable changes to their diet and lifestyle, thereby reducing the burden on healthcare systems and minimising food waste.


Our Values:

At NutriHelix we prioritise the health and wellbeing of our clients above all else. Our values of community, justice, respect of diversity, beliefs and cultural background underpin everything we do. We also take social and environmental responsibility very seriously by giving back to the community and taking steps to reduce our environmental impact.
