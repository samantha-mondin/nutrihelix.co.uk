Our Scientific Advisory Board

Our scientific advisory board is an integral part of our commitment to providing the highest quality nutrition services and products. We at NutriHelix are proud to have a team of highly qualified professionals on our scientific advisory board. Our board members are leading experts and researchers in the fields of nutrigenomics, personalised nutrition and health, with extensive knowledge and experience in their respective areas of expertise. They provide valuable guidance and support to our company, ensuring that our products and services are backed by the latest scientific research and promoting evidence-based practices.
They regularly collaborate with our team to refine existing services, provide educational resources for our customers and develop innovative and science-backed solutions to improve our customers' health and wellness.

Our Nutritional Cunsultants

At NutriHelix, we recognise that each individual has unique nutritional needs. Our team of experienced and knowledgeable nutritionists uses the latest research in nutrigenomics and nutrigenetics to assess your dietary needs. We provide you with personalised nutrition plans tailored to your specific genetic makeup, health status, nutritional biomarkers, body composition, and individual food preferences. Our consultants work with you to develop a plan that meets your unique needs and fits your lifestyle, whether you are looking to lose weight, manage a chronic health condition, or improve athletic performance.
At NutriHelix, we are committed to helping you achieve your health goals. Our team of nutritionists will be with you every step of the way, providing education and support to make sustainable changes to your diet and lifestyle.

Our Cyber Security Team

Our cybersecurity team consists of experienced professionals who are well-versed in a wide range of security technologies and methodologies. They conduct regular risk assessments and vulnerability scans, analyse security incidents, and respond to security incidents promptly and effectively. By having a specialised team focused solely on cybersecurity, we can ensure that we have the expertise and resources needed to protect our customers' data against the latest threats and attacks. This enables our customers to have the peace of mind that their information is in safe hands with us.
