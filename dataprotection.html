Our Commitment to Data Protection

At NutriHelix, we take data protection very seriously. We understand that in today's digital age, personal information is a valuable commodity that needs to be protected from cyber threats and unauthorized access. We also understand that the protection of your personal information is essential to maintaining your trust in our company. That's why we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection and cybersecurity to ensure that personal information is securely stored and protected from unauthorized access.

How do we protect our costumersâ€™ data?

  • •  NutriHeix collects only information that is necessary for the services we provide.

  • •  Our data protection policies are designed to meet GDPR standards, including obtaining explicit consent from customers for data processing, providing transparent information on how we use customer data, and ensuring the secure storage and transfer of data.

  • •  Strict access controls are in place, allowing only authorized personnel to access customer data, and access policies are regularly reviewed and updated.

  • •  NutriHelix employees receive regular training on data protection best practices to maintain a culture of security and awareness.

  • •  Customer data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and backup mechanisms to prevent unauthorized access or data loss.


In addition to our data protection policy, we have a dedicated cyber security team that works tirelessly to protect our systems from cyber threats. Our team uses the latest technology and industry best practices to safeguard your personal information and ensure that it is not compromised. We regularly conduct security assessments and penetration testing to identify and address any potential security risks and ensure that our systems remain secure.

If you have any questions or concerns about our data protection policies or procedures, please do not hesitate to contact us at: cyberteam@nutrihelix.uk
We are here to help and ensure that your personal information is protected at all times.

Our Cyber Security Team

Our cybersecurity team consists of experienced professionals who are well-versed in a wide range of security technologies and methodologies. They conduct regular risk assessments and vulnerability scans, analyse security incidents, and respond to security incidents promptly and effectively. By having a specialized team focused solely on cybersecurity, we can ensure that we have the expertise and resources needed to protect our customers' data against the latest threats and attacks. This enables our customers to have the peace of mind that their information is in safe hands with us.
