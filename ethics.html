Ethics

Ethics


At NutriHelix all our nutritionists are registered with UK Voluntary Register of Nutritionists (UKVRN), adhering to the Association for Nutition (AfN) Standards of Ethics, Conduct and Performance. This means that our nutritionists are held to the highest ethical and professional standards, providing the highest quality services to our customers.

At our company, we are committed to:

  • •  Working within our scope of practice to ensure the highest standards of professional conduct, level of expertise and quality of service for our customers

  • •  Prioritising our customers' health and wellbeing in all aspects of our work, avoiding any activities that may harm the health or wellbeing of our clients.

  • •  Maintaining accurate client records to ensure confidentiality and privacy.

  • •  Engaging in regular continuing professional development (CPD) to stay up-to-date with the latest industry knowledge and practices.

  • •  Ensuring a transparent and honest communication about our staff qualifications, expertise and experience.

  • •  Respecting the diversity and cultural backgrounds of our customers and convicting any discriminatory behaviour or language.

  • •  Refraning from making any false or deceptive statements.

  • •  Complying with relevant laws and regulations to ensure legal and ethical practices

  • •  Prioritising open and honest communication with our customers, employees, and shareholders to build trust and credibility.

Enviromental Responsability


At NutriHelix, we recognise the importance of environmental sustainability and are committed to contributing to safeguarding the environment by reducing our carbon footprint in all aspects of our business operations.
We believe that even small changes can make a significant difference, and we are proud to do our part to preserve the environment for future generations by implementing various initiatives.

At NutriHelix:

  • •  We partner with suppliers who share our commitment to sustainability to reduce the environmental impact of our supply chain.

  • •  We work with courier companies that use vehicles with low carbon emissions to transport our products.

  • •  We have implemented a waste management system that includes proper disposal of hazardous materials.

  • •  We prioritise recycled and sustainable materials for packaging our DNA kit boxes, avoiding and limiting the use of plastic.

  • •  We adopt eco-friendly practices such as telecommuting to reduce our carbon footprint, minimise waste, and reduce pollution.


At NutriHelix, we strive to make a positive impact on the environment through our commitment to sustainability, and we will continue to seek out new ways to minimise our environmental impact and promote environmental responsibility in all aspects of our business.

Social Responsability


At NutriHelix, we are committed to social responsibility and believe in giving back to our community. We recognize that many individuals and families in our community struggle with food insecurity, especially during times of economic hardship.
To address this issue, we have partnered with The Trussell Trust, a nationwide network of food banks, to distribute food to those who need it most. We make regular donations and volunteer our time to support these efforts, as we believe that access to healthy and nutritious food is a fundamental human right.
We are proud to support our community in this way and will continue to prioritise social responsibility as a core value of our company. By working together to support those in need, we can create a better future for all.


At NutriHelix, we are deeply committed to supporting and improving the health and lives of people living with scleroderma. We recognise the critical role that proper nutrition plays in managing the symptoms of this disease, including gastrointestinal involvement, especially among people living with diffuse scleroderma (also known as systemic sclerosis).
We are very proud to partner with the Scleroderma and Raynaud's UK Association (SRUK) to offer free personalised nutritional consultations to patients affected by systemic sclerosis.
In addition, we make regular donations to the Scleroderma Research Foundation and GILS (Gruppo Italiano per la lotta alla Sclerodermia) to support scientific research on scleroderma.
We believe that by supporting research, we can improve our understanding of this disease and develop new treatments that will improve the lives of patients.
At NutriHelix, we are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of those affected by scleroderma, and we will continue to support this important cause through ongoing partnerships, initiatives, and research.
